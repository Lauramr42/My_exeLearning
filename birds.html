Birds

     Birds have the following characteristics:

  • Birds can regulate their body temperature. They are called warm-blooded (endothermic).
  • They don't have teeth, and they present toothless bills/beaks.
  • Birds are born from eggs.
  • All their body is covered with feathers, being the only animals, which have them.
  • Some birds' skeleton is composed of porous lightweight bones (to help them fly).
  • Birds can fly using wings, and live most of their lives in the air.
