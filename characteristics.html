Characteristics

     Some important characteristics of the Animal Kingdom are:

  • They are formed by more than one cell.
  • They feed on other beings through ingestion.
  • They breathe oxygen (most of them).
  • Animals have sexual reproduction.

Other classification

     Depending on their diet (what they eat), animals can be classified into:

  • Carnivorous. Those animals that eat other animals.
  • Herbivorous. Those animals that eat plants.
  • Omnivorous. Those animals who eat both animals and plants.
