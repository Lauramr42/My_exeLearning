THE KINGDOMS OF LIFE

Do you think all living beings are equal?

     What do you see in the pictures?

     In order to study living beings, researchers have classified and categorized them, from the smallest one to the biggest, depending on some characteristics. There have been stablished different classifications, according to new scientific discoveries and expert positions.

     Some of the aspects that are considered to classify living beings within one kingdom or another are the type of cell, how do they feed, how do they reproduce, etc. There are six kingdoms that scientists recognise at the moment, even though, there were others in the past. Each one of them has defining characteristics that help with the classification:   

  • ANIMALS
  • PLANTS
  • FUNGI
  • PROTOZOA
  • PROTISTA
  • BACTERIA
