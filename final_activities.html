Compare and contrast

     There are aspects and characteristics that make plants and animals part of different kingdoms.

     Anylize the information about the Animal Kingdom and Plant Kingdom. Using thtat information, create a Venn Diagram to compare and contrast both kingdoms.

Look, think and say

Look at the images. Analyze each picture and write down their characteristics. Then classify those living being according to those characteristicss.

Write your results and expose your classification of one of them to the rest of the class.
