Reptiles

     Reptiles have the following characteristics:

  • Reptiles cannot regulate and keep their body temperature, so they get their body heat from external sources. They are cold-blooded (exothermic).
  • They are the longest lived species on the planet. Tortoises can live more than 150 years.
  • Their bodies are covered with scales, or they have bony external plate (shell)
  • Reptiles cannot chew their food, they can only tear
  • Most reptiles lay eggs, although some lizards are viviparous.
  • Some of them are terrestrial, others live in water.
