Plants with flowers

     Plants are divided in two groups: plants with flowers (angiosperms) and plants without flowers.

     Flowering plants are those with complex flashy flowers. Their seeds are covered with fruits that protect them. Plants from this group have the following characteristics:

  • They have flowers. They can be big or small.
  • They have fruits. Inside them there are seeds, which have the genetic information for a new plant, and the nutrients it needs to grow.
  • They can be herbs, trees or bushes.
