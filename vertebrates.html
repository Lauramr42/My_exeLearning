Vertebrates

     Vertebrate animals have internal skeleton. All of them have a backbone, which is a structure that surrounds and protects the spinal cord. The spinal cord connects all nerves in the body to the brain.

     Their body is divided into three different parts:

  • Head
  • Trunk
  • Extremities or legs.

     They are classified in 5 groups:

  • MAMMALS
  • BIRDS
  • FISH
  • REPTILES
  • AMPHIBIOUS
