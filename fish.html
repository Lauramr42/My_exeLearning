Fish

     Fish have the following characteristics:

  • They generally live all their life underwater, although some fish can live out of it (mudskippers).
  • Fish have gills throughout its life cycle to breath.
  • Many fish have scales covering all their bodies. There are many types of scales, and they shield the body from the environment. They also improve their movements in the water.
  • Fins are anatomic parts of fish. Their general purpose is to provide motion and maneuverability.
  • Almost all fish are cold-blooded animals, as they cannot regulate their body temperature (exothermic).
