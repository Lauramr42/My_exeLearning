Moss and Fern

     Both ferns and moss are plants without flowers. Because of it, they don't reproduce by fruits and seeds, but by spores. Spores are special cells which are surrounded with a resistant cover. They are the origin of new plants when they arrive to a different place, as they are spread by the wind and water.
